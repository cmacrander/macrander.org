Web Architect; Education Researcher

I lead software development for a PERTS, a research group at Stanford University. I have five years of experience building full stack web applications with user bases in the tens of thousands, using a variety of open source technologies. The most important lesson I've learned in that time is that problem-focused design is hard, takes time, and is enormously rewarding when it's done right.

Building software is a welcome albeit unexpected niche for me. I trained as an elementary school teacher, and have lead several classrooms through their p's and q's over the years. I maintain a feeling of relevance in the work I do by constantly tying it back to education, and I'm always looking for ways to get involved with the experience of students.
