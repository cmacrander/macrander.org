Sadie Anderson

A collection of works in paper and paint

From the time when I was very young, I have loved creating art. I love the process- being inspired by something I see, someone I know, something someone says, and expanding that into an image. I love the actual craft of creating- holding the brush or knife and building something that expresses my idea.

I particularly love creating custom work that will mean something special to its owner. Because my focus is on custom work, I am happy to work with you to create a piece that fits your desires and budget. Please do not hesitate to contact me with questions or thoughts, I would love to hear from you!
